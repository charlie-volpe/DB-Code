• Daniel Burwen is an award winning Creative Director and thought leader on interactive narrative. He has presented internationally at venues such as SXSW, San Diego Comicon, and IDFA.
  • He has over 10 years experience working across a variety of creative industries from design to console games (EA Games, Activision), to interactive media (Disney, Lucasfilm).

  • He lives in San Francisco and proudly serves as a judge for the Webby Awards.
