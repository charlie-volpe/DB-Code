The Four Elements of Interactive Stories

Story

It all starts with a script. Plot. Character. Theme. Good story is the core of your experience, the foundation upon which the other elements are built.

User Experience

Good UX will ensure your audience is spending their time engrossed in an emotional experience and not stuck trying to decipher an interface.

Aesthetics

How is it presented? From film to comics to 3D, good cohesive aesthetics are critical to achieving the right emotional response from your audience.

Technology

The key to creating great software is great tools and technology. Picking the right tech will ensure you can tell your stories in the best way possible.

Our Services

We can do wonders for your interactive projects. Here's how.
