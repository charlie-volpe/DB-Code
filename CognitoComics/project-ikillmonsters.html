I Kill Monsters

When we set out to craft the next
generation of comics books,
we had no
idea we would end up building our
content
inside a 3D engine.

I Kill Monsters is the first true hybrid
format of realtime 3D games and moving
comics, seamlessly interwoven together
into an entirely new storytelling fomat.

Currently in development
internally at Cognito.
